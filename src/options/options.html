Customize the aging dots on your Jira boards.

Hide dots in these status categories

Jira maps every column to one of three status categories. Hiding by category works regardless of column names.

About

This extension hides Jira's native aging dots in the status categories you select, and recomputes the count using working days only. All data stays in your browser; no third-party services are contacted.

Tip: changes apply immediately to any open Jira tab.
